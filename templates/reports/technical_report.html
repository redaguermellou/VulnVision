5. Remediation Plan

Addressing the findings should follow a risk-based approach, prioritizing Critical and High severity issues. We recommend the following steps:

  1. Patch systems and applications affected by the known CVEs immediately.
  2. Ensure all unnecessary open ports and services are blocked or disabled.
  3. Apply secure configuration baselines to web servers and database systems.
  4. Re-run the VulnVision scan to verify remediation effectiveness.

6. Appendix

Terms and Definitions:

  • Critical: Immediate threat to system integrity, confidentiality, or availability. Exploitation is usually easy and requires no user interaction.
  • High: Significant risk of exploitation. May require some prerequisites but can lead to severe data compromise or system control.
  • Medium: Flaws that could potentially be exploited, often requiring a complex scenario or prior authentication.
  • Low: Informational issues, minor misconfigurations, or theoretical weaknesses requiring exceptional circumstances to exploit.
