1. Introduction to PCI-DSS Scope

The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) dictates strict requirements for environments that store, process, or transmit cardholder data. VulnVision's automated vulnerability scanning specifically assists organizations in meeting Requirement 11 (Regularly testing security systems and processes).
